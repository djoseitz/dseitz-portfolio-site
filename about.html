About Me

My name is Dave Seitz, and I’m a Full-Stack Web Developer. I spent several years in high-level customer success and support positions, before transitioning to web development. In my previous roles I have solved complex customer problems, coordinated projects, and trained new teammates. I developed a deep understanding of customer needs, and became highly skilled at translating those needs into achievable project goals.

Whilst I have always enjoyed coordinating projects, I decided I wanted to transition to a role where I could create things, rather than simply put out fires. I’ve always been interested in the versatility and creative problem-solving of web development, and did a bit of it in the past, but the time was right to commit to a full transition. I’m looking to join a team where my skills in web design, coordination, and project management can translate into a fantastic product.

Since making this transition I’ve developed a number of projects, ranging from my own take on a Pokedex for the popular game Pokemon, to designing and building both the database and user interface for a web app for storing information on my favourite movies. In building these projects, I’ve developed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React as well as server-side development tools such as SQL and AWS.
